Training Modules
Complete in order
01
X32 Board Anatomy — Front Panel
Learn every zone on the X32 front panel. Study mode covers each section's function in depth. Quiz mode tests your ability to identify zones on the actual board.
Quiz ~20 min · 17 questions →
02
X32 Board Anatomy — Rear Panel
Every connection on the rear panel — inputs, outputs, CAT6 to stage, recording feeds, IEM sends, and AES50. Know where every cable goes and why.
Quiz ~15 min · 14 questions →
03
Science of Sound
Frequency spectrum, instrument carving, and why you cut before you boost. The physics behind what you hear in the room and how to fix it at the board.
Quiz ~15 min · 12 questions →
04
Gain Structure
The single most important concept in live sound. Understand why gain structure determines everything downstream — and how to set it correctly every time.
Quiz ~12 min · 10 questions →
05
EQ & Compression
Troubleshoot by ear. Match symptoms to frequency ranges. Apply compression correctly for drums vs. vocals and understand why the settings differ.
Quiz ~18 min · 14 questions →
06
IEM Monitor Mixes
Pre-fader vs. post-fader, building a monitor mix with Sends on Fader, and the golden rule of in-ear monitoring. What a performer hears matters as much as what the house hears.
Quiz ~12 min · 10 questions →
07
Service Flow
Scenario-based quiz. You are at FOH. The pastor walks to the mic. The altar call starts. A fader is wrong. What do you do — and when? Transitions are where mistakes happen.
Quiz ~15 min · 12 questions →
